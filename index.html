Welcome to Krishnamurty World School

The concept behind Krishnamurty World School imbibes into itself the globally acceptable ideology of Lord Krishna which has perennial and infinite application. In line with the Lord's ideology, the school believes in "NISKAM KARMA", the continuous performance without expectation of outcome. The school considers that the result is quite obvious if duty is fully complied with.

In appreciation to the message underlying the Bhagwat Gita, the inspiring words of Lord Krishna, the school aims at preparing its students in such a way that they can through self reliance face the challenges of time and standup for themselves.
